Computational and Experimental Analysis of Early Reflections in Concert Spaces

The presented work builds 3D models for four real rooms in the Schulich School of Music with Matlab and calculates their early reflection responses with given sources and receivers. The results are then compared with the measured impulse responses regarding amplitude, time and echo densities.
